Our Story

Founded in 2005, Sari's Restaurant brings the authentic flavors of Canadian cuisine to the heart of the city. Our journey began with a simple passion for good food and warm hospitality.

Today, we continue that tradition by sourcing the finest local ingredients and preparing each dish with the care and attention it deserves. Every meal at Sari's tells a story of Canada's rich culinary heritage.
